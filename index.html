What is the powerhouse of the cell?

What are the four different biomolecules?
Carbohydrates, Proteins, nuclia acids, and lipids
What are proteins made of?
Amino acids
What is a polysaccharide?
A carbohydrate made of two or more monosaccharides
What is a monosaccharide?
The simplest unit of a carbohydrate
What is the difference between adhesion and cohesion?
Adhesion describes the attraction between molecules of different substances while cohesion is the attraction of molecules of different substance
What causes a molecule to be polar?
Molecules that have a dipole or an uneven distribution of charge resulting in one side being positive and the other negative
What is a carbohydrate and what is it made from?
A sugar or starch used to store energy
